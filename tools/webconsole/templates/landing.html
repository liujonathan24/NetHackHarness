> NetHack Web Console

An interactive browser front-end over the fork NetHack engine (EngineEnv). Play the dungeon live, then dial the difficulty and generation knobs in real time: Vision (night-vision radius, fog-of-war, full-map reveal), Stat-based scaling (damage dealt/taken, HP, regen, hunger, XP gain), and Dungeon & spawns (room density, monster difficulty, ongoing spawn rate, monster speed). Live knobs apply immediately; reset knobs regenerate the floor on Reset.

Sessions can be recorded as .ndjson traces and replayed turn-by-turn in the Tracer — scrub the map, status, reward, messages, and any LLM agent panes. The gallery below shows a few knobs in action.
